### Platform-Specific Optimization
Detailed specs for each platform:

#### Instagram
- Feed: 1080x1350 (4:5), carousel up to 10 slides
- Reels: 1080x1920 (9:16), 15-90s, cover image 1080x1920
- Stories: 1080x1920, 15s segments, sticker/poll engagement
- Caption: 2200 char max, first 125 chars visible, 20-30 hashtags (hidden in comments)
- Best performing content types: carousels > reels > single image

#### TikTok
- Video: 1080x1920 (9:16), 15s-10min (sweet spot: 21-34s)
- On-screen text hooks in first 1-2s
- Trending sounds integration
- 3-5 hashtags max, mix trending + niche
- Duet/stitch strategies

#### YouTube
- Long-form: 1920x1080 (16:9), 8-20min optimal
- Shorts: 1080x1920 (9:16), under 60s
- Thumbnail: 1280x720, high contrast, face + text, 3 elements max
- Title: 60 chars max, front-load keywords
- Description: first 2 lines visible, include timestamps, links, keywords
- Tags: 5-15 relevant tags
- End screens and cards placement

#### X/Twitter
- Text: 280 chars, thread strategy for long content
- Images: up to 4, 1200x675 or 1200x1200
- Video: up to 2:20, 1280x720 minimum
- Best times for engagement, reply threading

#### LinkedIn
- Posts: 3000 chars, first 3 lines hook
- Video: 1:1 or 4:5, 30s-5min
- Document posts (PDF carousels): up to 300 pages, 4:5 ratio
- Professional tone, industry insights

### Script Writing Patterns
- **Hook formulas** (first 3 seconds):
  - Question hook: "Did you know that...?"
  - Stat hook: "97% of people don't know..."
  - Controversy hook: "Stop doing X right now"
  - Story hook: "I was about to give up when..."
  - List hook: "3 things that changed my..."
- **AIDA Framework**: Attention -> Interest -> Desire -> Action
- **PAS Framework**: Problem -> Agitation -> Solution
- **BAB Framework**: Before -> After -> Bridge
- **StoryBrand**: Character -> Problem -> Guide -> Plan -> Action -> Success -> Failure
- CTA placement: end of video, pinned comment, bio link
- Engagement triggers: questions, polls, "comment X if you agree"

### A/B Testing
- What to test: thumbnails, titles, hooks, CTAs, posting times
- Sample size requirements: minimum 1000 impressions per variant before concluding
- Statistical significance: 95% confidence interval minimum
- Iteration workflow: test → analyze after 48h → implement winner → next test

### Hashtag Strategy by Platform

#### Instagram Hashtag Formula
- 3-5 broad hashtags (500K-5M posts): #marketing #business #entrepreneur
- 5-10 medium hashtags (50K-500K posts): #contentcreation #socialmediatips
- 5-10 niche hashtags (5K-50K posts): #aicontentcreation #brandstrategy2026
- 2-3 branded hashtags: #YourBrandName #YourCampaign
- Place in first comment, not caption (cleaner look, same reach)

#### TikTok Hashtag Formula
- 1-2 trending hashtags (check Discover page)
- 1-2 niche community hashtags
- 1 branded hashtag
- Total: 3-5 max (more looks spammy)

#### YouTube Tags
- Primary keyword as first tag
- Long-tail keyword variations
- Competitor video titles as tags
- 15-20 tags total, most relevant first

### Engagement Optimization Tactics

#### The 3-Second Rule
Every piece of content must hook in 3 seconds or less:
- **Visual hook**: Unexpected image, fast motion, bright colors, text on screen
- **Audio hook**: Sound effect, provocative statement, question
- **Pattern interrupt**: Something that breaks scrolling behavior

#### Engagement Triggers
1. **Ask a question**: "What would you do?" "Which one is better?"
2. **Create controversy**: Take a stance, challenge common belief
3. **Use "save this"**: Trigger saves (strongest algorithmic signal)
4. **Open loops**: "Wait for the end..." (boosts watch time)
5. **Call to comment**: "Tag someone who needs this"
6. **Share value**: Actionable tips people want to share with others
